Services

Kitchens and bathrooms, in full detail.

Two specialties, each covered end to end — from first layout sketch to the final wipe-down before handover.

Kitchen Design

The kitchen carries more daily wear than any other room. We plan it as one continuous surface — cabinetry, stone, and appliances resolved together, not chosen separately.

  • Layout & Millwork
  • Benchtops & Splashbacks
  • Lighting Design
  • Appliance Integration
  • Island & Storage Planning
  • Hardware & Finishes

Bathroom Design

Bathrooms combine plumbing, waterproofing, and finish in a small footprint. We detail every junction so the room performs quietly for decades.

  • Tiling & Waterproofing
  • Fixtures & Fittings
  • Vanity & Storage
  • Wet-Room Detailing
  • Heated Flooring & Ventilation
  • Mirror & Lighting Plans

How We Work

Our process

The same five stages carry every project, kitchen or bathroom, from a first idea to a finished room.

01

Consultation

We visit the space, talk through how you use it, and set a budget and timeline you're comfortable with.

02

Concept & Materials

Layout options and a material palette — stone, timber, metal — presented as physical samples, not just renders.

03

Detailed Design

Cabinetry drawings, fixture schedules, and lighting plans detailed enough for tradespeople to build from directly.

04

Build Oversight

We stay on site through demolition, rough-in, and installation, checking work against the drawings at each stage.

05

Reveal

A final walkthrough and snag list before handover, with care notes for every surface and finish we've specified.
